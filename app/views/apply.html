Apply to join the IAX

If you want to be an identity provider on the Identity and Attributes Exchange (IAX) you need to apply to join.

Find out more about the benefits of joining the IAX.

Once you’ve completed the pre-application checklist, and your auditor has confirmed you meet the criteria, you should apply to join the IAX.

You’ll need to include:

  • your contact details
  • company information
  • details about your service

Once your application has been approved, you can get certified.
