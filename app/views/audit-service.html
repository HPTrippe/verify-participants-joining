Audit a service that wants to join the trust framework

The trust framework is a shared set of rules that makes it possible for companies that provide digital identity services to work with another.

Before you can provide your digital identity service on the trust framework, you need to get certified by an independent auditor.

If you are an auditor or want to be an auditor, use the links below to download all the documents that will help you assess a digital identity service:
