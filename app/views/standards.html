Standards and rules you must follow

You must make sure you understand the responsibilities of an identity provider in the Identity and Attributes Exchange (IAX).

You must also meet all of the following standards and rules to be part of the IAX.

  • How to prove and verify someone's identity
  • Using authenticators to protect an online service
  • How to manage your records
  • Data protection in the IAX
  • Identity Assurance Principles

As part of the certification process, your auditor will also give you the:

  • base profile
  • identify provider profile

You'll need to show the auditor evidence that you meet these profiles.
