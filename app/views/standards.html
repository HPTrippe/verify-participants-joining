Standards and rules you must follow

You must meet all of the following standards and rules if you want to be part of the trust framework:

  • Identity proofing and verfication of an individual
  • Using credentials to protect an online service (opens in Dropbox Paper)
  • Manage identity risks to your service
  • Understanding, sharing and maintaining attributes
  • How to describe your attributes
  • Transaction monitoring for HMG online service providers
  • Privacy rules of the trust framework
  • Identity Assurance Principles

Before you start your application, youâ€™ll need to send some evidence to your auditor that proves you meet some of these standards.

Depending on what role you want to do in the trust framework, you must also meet any of the following:

  • the identity provider profile
  • the attribute provider profile
  • the broker profile

These profiles will help you build your service in a way that follows the standards.
