Join and participate in the trust framework

  1. 1. Find out about the trust framework

    The trust framework is a shared set of rules that makes it possible for companies that provide digital identity services to work with another.

    • Usability guidelines

  5. 5. Get certified

    • Get certified to be part of the trust framework

  6. 6. Sign contract and join

    After you've signed the contract, you must:

    • join the trust framework directory
    • connect to eIDAS (if you're a broker)

  7. 7. Participate

    You must always follow the guidance on being a participant. There are different rules to follow if you want to be:

    • an identity provider (opens as PDF)
    • an attribute provider
    • a broker

    There are other things you must do if you want to leave the trust framework.
