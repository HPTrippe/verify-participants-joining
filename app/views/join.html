Join the Identity and Attributes Exchange (IAX) as an identity provider

As a member of the Identity and Attributes Exchange (IAX), you'll have access to information from other trusted organisations. You can find out about the other benefits of being part of the IAX before you join.

You can currently only join the IAX as an identity provider. You must follow these 3 steps to join. It usually takes a few months to complete them all.

  1. 1. Read and follow the rules

    Find out what standards and rules you must follow.

  2. 2. Build or adapt your service

    You can get ready to provide digital identities in the IAX by setting up an OpenID Connect profile.

  3. 3. Join the IAX and get certified

    Find out how to:

    • get certified to join the IAX
    • apply to join the IAX
