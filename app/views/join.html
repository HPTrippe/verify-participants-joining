Join the Identity and Attributes Exchange (IAX) as an identity provider

As a member of the IAX, you'll have access to information from other trusted organisations.

There are 4 main steps to becoming an identity provider. It usually takes a few months to complete them all.

  1. 1. Read and follow the rules

    Find out what standards and rules you must follow.

  2. 2. Build or adapt your service

    You can get ready to provide digital identities in the IAX by setting up an OpenID Connect profile.

  3. 3. Get certified

    You must be certified by an auditor before you can join the IAX.

  4. 4. Apply

    You must:

    • fill in an application form
    • enrol your organisation in the directory
